Cybersigil tattoo trio

Cybersigilism is a tattoo style that features thin lines, sharp angles and usually black ink to create intricate patterns that resembles a mix of circuitry and organic forms. These three unique tattoos were created for the Young Ones' competition submission. Each tattoo is a stand alone design but together they form a cohesive series.
