Ride the rave - Social media ad

I had to create a social media ad for a pre-existing company and I chose ride.therave - An underground rave organization that hosts techno club nights at locations all over Vancouver. I wanted to create an illustration that evokes how RTR Promotes inclusivity, not being judged and having a release and how it is such a safe and welcoming environment where music is truly appreciated.
