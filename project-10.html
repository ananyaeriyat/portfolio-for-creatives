Sea-keepers:
Gaining Ground | Video game concept

Sea-Keepers: Gaining Ground is a story-driven game that immerses players in a future where 70% of the land is underwater. As part of three specialized teams—above, below, and in-between—players must explore, adapt, and work together to prevent the collapse of Earth’s last remaining land. Blending strategy and environmental themes, this game challenges players to think critically about resilience and the power of collaboration.
