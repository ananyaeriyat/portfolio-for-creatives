Adidas Mural project

North Vancouver, and all of Canada, is known for it's famous wildlife squad: The Black bear,the ever-present seagull and the the infamous raccon and squirrel. WIth this mural I aimed to create an artwork that would connect with people of all ages who love the classic " Gazelle" sneaker. The goal was to make clear that if the local Black bear can rock the Gazelle sneaker so can you.
