Sea-keepers:Digital illustration!

Two sea wolves and a grizzly bear team up to research, maintain and protect the biodiversity in the ocean. They go by the name, “ Sea keepers.” with a capital K. The grizzly bear is an oceanographer and usually stays on the shore since he gets seasick. This project focuses on the two sea wolves : An older female marine biologist and her younger brother, a marine conservation diver. Like any sibling relationship they sometimes end up in dangerous waters.

This composition of this illustration is purposefully created to work as both a 1920x1080 and a 1080x1080 illustration.
