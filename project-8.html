Cornucopia | Mural illustration | brand development

Cornucopia is a slow fashion brand dedicated to preventing food waste. Our natural dyes are made from discarded produce purchased from grocery stores. 50% of all profits go back into purchasing food for the community, giving back to food banks and homeless shelters. Cornucopia ensures that our production line is clean all the way through, using eco-friendly materials and ensuring traceable supply chains.
