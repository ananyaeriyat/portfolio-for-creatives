Upstairs Neighbours

Upstairs Neighbours blends humor, transformation, and slice-of-life storytelling in a quirky two-page spread. This lighthearted graphic novel follows a werewolf’s nocturnal adventures with a knowing roommate, the oblivious third roommate’s complaints about “loud upstairs neighbors,” and the chaos that ensues when the truth hits too close to home.
